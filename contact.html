Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it typically take to get a response?

Our team strives to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For more complex questions or during peak periods, it may take up to 72 hours. If you haven't received a response within this timeframe, please check your spam folder or contact us again.

Can I submit my own recipes for publication?

Yes! We welcome recipe submissions from our community. Please use the contact form and select "Recipe Inquiry" as the subject. Include your recipe, high-quality photos, and a brief story about the dish. Our editorial team reviews all submissions and will contact you if we're interested in featuring your recipe.

Do you offer culinary classes or workshops?

We periodically host workshops, cooking classes, and culinary events. These are announced on our website and social media channels. We also collaborate with culinary schools and chefs for special events. If you're interested in attending or hosting a workshop, please contact us with "Collaboration Request" as the subject.

Can I use your recipes for my restaurant or cookbook?

Our recipes are protected by copyright, but we often grant permissions for non-commercial use with proper attribution. For commercial use (restaurants, cookbooks, etc.), please contact us with details about your project. We're usually happy to collaborate and may offer licensing options.

Do you have physical locations or stores?

Currently, Lucent Gastronomic Nexus operates primarily as an online resource. However, we occasionally participate in food festivals, farmers markets, and culinary events. Our test kitchen (located at the address above) is not open to the public, but we sometimes host special events for newsletter subscribers and partners.
